Welcome

This is the galactic marketplace, where you can buy and trade raw elements, and start your own mining operations to provide long term profits. The Galaxy needs your materials, go deliver them!

How it works

You gather elements by mining and trading with other companies. Then find planets with the best price to sell to!

The more you sell to a planet the less they will pay until they put out new orders for materials, you'll have no idea when this will be however so take advantage of good prices while they are there. Just remember finding the best price takes time, letting other players buy from you can leave you with more time to decide what ship your going to purchase next!
